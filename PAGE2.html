EFFECTIVE COMMUNICATION

NARRATION

The article discusses the importance of preparing before writing a research paper or scientific manuscript. It explains that effective writing starts with having a clear purpose, identifying the main message, and understanding the audience. The article emphasizes that writers should carefully plan their work, develop a strong research question, and organize their ideas before they begin writing. These steps help ensure that information is communicated clearly and effectively.

The article also highlights the importance of choosing the right journal and following its guidelines. Different journals have different audiences and requirements, so writers must adapt their work accordingly. In addition, the article provides resources and tips that can help researchers improve their writing skills and communicate their findings in a professional manner. Overall, the article shows that effective communication is essential for sharing knowledge and making research understandable to others.

REFLECTION

After reading the article, I realized that effective communication involves more than just writing words on a page. It requires careful planning, organization, and consideration of the audience. The article helped me understand that presenting ideas clearly is important because it allows readers to easily understand the message being shared.

The article encouraged me to improve my communication skills, especially when working on school projects and research activities. I learned that being organized and thoughtful in the way I present information can make a big difference in how others understand my work. In the future, I will apply these lessons by expressing my ideas more clearly and preparing carefully before writing.
