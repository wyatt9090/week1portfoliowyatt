GENERAL SCIENCE:TURBINE

NARRATION

The video explains how wind turbines work and how they convert the kinetic energy of moving air into electrical energy. When the wind blows, it causes the turbine blades to rotate. The rotating blades spin a shaft connected to a generator, which then produces electricity. The video also introduces the main parts of a wind turbine and explains how each component contributes to the generation of power.

In addition, the video discusses the benefits and challenges of wind energy. Wind power is a renewable and environmentally friendly source of energy that helps reduce pollution and dependence on fossil fuels. However, the amount of electricity generated depends on the availability and strength of the wind. Despite these challenges, wind energy continues to be an important source of clean power in many parts of the world.

REFLECTION
